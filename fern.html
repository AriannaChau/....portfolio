Fern

Fern was a two day project completed at Epicodus. With pair programming, one pair would give another an idea to make a site out of and the other pair would complete it. My partner, Karina Roush, and I were given a subscription box idea, where women could go and take a quiz about makeup and close and have items handpicked for them and shipped to their house. We went through user stories, created personas and we interviewed other women in our cohort to get opinions on what they would expect from a website like this.
